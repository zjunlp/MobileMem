1. **Natural and authentic**: Messages should reflect casual, real-life chat habits
2. **Event-related**: The conversation should relate to the described event
3. **Each person's speech must strictly match their persona**:

   - Peers (friends, colleagues) are more casual, use jokes and slang

4. **Main character participates**: The main person ({main_person_name}) must participate, and their speech should match their personality traits
5. **side field**: Messages from the main person should have side="right", others side="left"
6. **Real names**: Use the exact member names from the group list. Do NOT invent new names.
